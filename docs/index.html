Anagram Hunt

A random word is displayed, and the player must list all its valid anagrams within a time limit. If the player finds every correct permutation before time runs out, they see a success screen with their elapsed time; if not, they see a list of all possible answers

Math Fact Practice

The player selects a mode (Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, or Division). A new problem appears, and the player has 30 seconds to solve as many consecutive equations as possible. Each correct answer increments the score, and when time expires, the final score is displayed.
